ABOUT US

PoliticMotion aim at solving political polarization around the world.

We believe that, through understanding how media affect people's emotion, people can better understand that emotion is easily triggered by media, and it does not represent the full self.

We also believe that, by talking to people with same or opposite opinions, people will start to realize that, behind the keyboard, we are all the same.

We are all just humans.
